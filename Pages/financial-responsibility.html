FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Alveary Costs:
A yearly membership to the Alveary is required for each family. With our co-op discount this is $250. The Alveary is a comprehensive curriculum, with the exception of a math curriculum. Curriculum for Grades 1-12th are provided with this cost.


Approximate Pillar and Oak Costs:
Last year each student’s total cost was $220. This included a $100 school membership fee, $95 book & supply fee, and $25 facility fee. Totals should be similar this year. (Ages 2-under pay the facility fee only)


Classroom Books:
Each student will need to bring their required class books to school at the beginning of the year. These books will be used in class and then sent home at the end of each term. Pillar and Oak will not keep these books. Siblings in the same form can share books.


Estimation of book costs:
Grade 1- $60, Form 1 - $60, Form 2 - $150, Form 3 - $175
